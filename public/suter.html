Internship Opportunity

My education at Iowa gave me the opportunity to work an internship over summer 2023. I ended up working for Genova Technologies in Cedar Rapids, and I enjoyed my time there. Genova was contracting to John Deere at the time to provide embedded software engineering solutions to their latest line of combines. My team, the Green Reapers, was tasked with implementing new functionality to grain headers and I spent a lot of time immersed in their Agile-based system. Bench Testing, Code Reviews, Writing Tests, were all important experiences I intent to bring into my professional career. Yet, my favorite experience was my trip to Silvis where I got to spend my day with the combines for testing. Those machines are a lot bigger up close. Overall, it was quite a positive experience and I look forward to starting a career doing much of the same work.
